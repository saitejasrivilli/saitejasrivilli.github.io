CTMap: LLM-Enabled Connectivity-Aware Path Planning in Millimeter-Wave Digital Twin Networks

Authors: Including Sai Teja Srivillibhutturu

IEEE International Conference on Communications (ICC) - CQRM Workshop 2026

Abstract: This paper presents CTMap, a novel framework that leverages Large Language Models for connectivity-aware path planning in millimeter-wave (mmWave) digital twin networks. By integrating LLM-based decision pipelines with OpenStreetMap data and Sionna 6G digital twin simulations, CTMap enables intelligent path planning that considers network connectivity constraints.

TopGPT: Cross-Encoder Enhanced Retrieval-Augmented Generation for Technical Document Question Answering in Telecommunications

Authors: Including Sai Teja Srivillibhutturu

Ongoing Research

Abstract: This research explores the development of TopGPT, a novel cross-encoder enhanced retrieval-augmented generation system designed specifically for technical document question answering in the telecommunications domain. By leveraging advanced cross-encoder re-ranking mechanisms combined with RAG architectures, TopGPT aims to improve the accuracy and relevance of responses to complex technical queries in telecommunications documentation.
