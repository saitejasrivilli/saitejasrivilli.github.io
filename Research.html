CTMap: LLM-Enabled Connectivity-Aware Path Planning in Millimeter-Wave Digital Twin Networks

Authors: Including Sai Teja Srivillibhutturu

IEEE International Conference on Communications (ICC) - CQRM Workshop 2026

Abstract: This paper presents CTMap, a novel framework that leverages Large Language Models for connectivity-aware path planning in millimeter-wave (mmWave) digital twin networks. By integrating LLM-based decision pipelines with OpenStreetMap data and Sionna 6G digital twin simulations, CTMap enables intelligent path planning that considers network connectivity constraints.
